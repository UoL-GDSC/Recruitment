Description

Being a Public Relation at the GDSC UoL means that you will be in charged of everything related to communication. 

The PR will be our main connection point when it comes to external networking, i.e. collaboration with other communities or clubs, connecting with potential sponsors or speakers. 

You will be responsible for building the foundation trust and come up with a plan for the collaboration at the initial state. 

This is a role that will work on a lot of behind-the-scenes work, working closely with the Lead. 

Requirements

  • Have decent semi-professional email writing etiquette
  • Have decent communication skills in English

Preferred

  • Have a good reputation online (i.e. Slack)
  • Have some connections with local communities (i.e. GDSCs, GDGs, University tech clubs)
