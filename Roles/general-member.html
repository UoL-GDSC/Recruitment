Description

This role is open for those willing to learn but have not yet excelled in any of the opened vacancies. 

Being a general core team member, you have the option to choose a pathway or specialization to persue. In this case, you will be learning, helping, and doing things mostly related to that area with the Lead and the person in that position. 

However, everyone in the team will be supporting each other if things came up. Most of the times, general core team member will come into play. 

This is the place for you to learn some skills for running a community and put it into practice, if you don't have the skills but have time and passion, you're very welcome!

Requirements

  • Not confident in any of the listed positions but have passion to pursue one of them  OR  Willing to learn everything about running a community but lack the skills and hands-on experience
  • Be aware of: "This is a student club, I'm here to learn and have fun, not here to work."

Preferred

  • Interested in joining the next GDSC UoL Core Team as well
  • Not currently work, just fully enjoying your student life
