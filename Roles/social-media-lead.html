Description

Being a Social Media Manager at the GDSC UoL means that you will be in charged of everything related to promotion and marketing in the club. 

The Social Media Manager will not be working on their own but with the Leads and maybe some general core team member that wanted to learn how to become one like you most of the time. 

Your responsibility will include: making posters, coming up with event slogans, creating events on the community platform, promoting our activity on various social media platforms. 

Requirements

  • Have decent knowledge or hands-on experience with using your chosen image editing software (i.e. PS, Canva)
  • Comfortable with putting yourself out on the internet when promoting
  • Good at keeping various accounts in tact and safe

Preferred

  • Have some experience with video editing and various streaming softwares (i.e. OBS, YouTube studio)
  • Fast response rate during ongoing events
  • Have a good reputation online (i.e. Slack)
