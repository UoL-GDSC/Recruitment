Description

Being a Vice Lead at the GDSC UoL means you'll take on a lot of responsibilities, more than any other roles other than the Lead. 

When someone happens to be not available, Vice Lead will be in the way to help and take over the task. 

This position has to work close with the Lead to provide support and help with decision making. I want someone that treats this as a club (and not work!) to be part of the Core Team, but especially the Vice Lead. 

Requirements

  • Able to lead and be responsible, take on work if others are not available
  • Able to support the Lead in the team, help make decisions, help with administration related tasks

Preferred

  • Interested in becoming the next GDSC UoL Lead
  • Befriend with the Lead (not just work-colleque relationship)
  • Gain satisfaction by contributing and supporting others
