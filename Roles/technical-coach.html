Description

Being a Technical Coach at the GDSC UoL means that you will be in charged of technical knowledge and hands-on experience related activities. 

Depending on your expertise, you may be given a different title after being offered a position. 

Your responsibility will also be specified during the onboarding process. 

More information about types of activities we might want from you can be seen in the application stage. 

Requirements

  • Have decent knowledge or hands-on experience in a certain area in tech
  • Have passion to share your knowledge with others

Preferred

  • Have worked in the industry in the past
  • Have good communication skills within your expertise domain
  • Have a response rate within 24 hours at all times
