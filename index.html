What are Google Developer Student Clubs?


Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) is initiative created by Google gathering college and university students interested in Google technologies and platforms. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs are welcome! By joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
